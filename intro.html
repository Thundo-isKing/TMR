Mobile quick-start (iPhone)

  • Use Safari: open the app link in Safari for the smoothest setup.
  • Add to Home Screen: Share → “Add to Home Screen” for a more app-like experience (this allows notifications).
  • Notifications: iOS support depends on version and whether the site is installed to Home Screen.
If notifications don’t work on iPhone, turn into webapp by adding to Home Screen and re-check site permissions(visual tutorial coming soon).

Mobile quick-start (Android)

  • Use Chrome: open the app link in Chrome/Google for the smoothest setup.
  • Enable Notifications: Allow notifications via popup.
  • Add to Home Screen: Share → “Add to Home Screen” for a more app-like experience (Needn't have to worry about recieving notifications... apple sucks).
